About PyLadies

PyLadies is an international mentorship group for marginalized genders, such as but not limited to non-binary people, trans people, and women in tech, with a focus on helping our community become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community. All skill levels welcome!

Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings.

To learn more, visit the PyLadies website.

Why should you register to be a PyLadies member?

Registering to be a member takes only a few minutes, and is free! Some benefits of being a PyLadies member include:

  • Gain automatic Python Software Foundation membership
  • Choose between basic or a voting membership to vote in the PyLadies elections
  • Offer feedback on the creation of PyLadies programming
  • Stay in touch with PyLadies!

FAQ

I don't see my PyLadies chapter listed in the membership options, why?

That means your PyLadies chapter hasn't registered in our Chapter Directory! Please let your PyLadies chapter organizers know they need to register. They can follow these directions. If the PyLadies chapter has more problems they can reach out in PyLadies Slack in the #project-admin-logistics channel.

How can I confirm if I am registered or which type of membership I have?

We are working on a PyLadies membership portal. For now, you can reach out in PyLadies Slack in the #project-admin-logistics channel.
